# Team Collaboration

## Collaboration principles

- Communicate early when scope, risk, or timelines change
- Share context, not just decisions
- Prefer transparent, documented discussions for durable decisions

## Working agreements

- Keep planning artifacts current and concise
- Use async updates to reduce meeting load
- Escalate blockers with clear asks and ownership

## Review culture

- Assume positive intent
- Focus feedback on code and design outcomes
- Close the loop by acknowledging and resolving comments

## Knowledge sharing

- Rotate ownership where practical
- Document key decisions and operational learnings
- Hold regular technical discussions to spread context
