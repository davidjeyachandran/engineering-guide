# Engineering Principles

## Why principles matter

Principles guide day-to-day decisions when requirements are ambiguous. They should be stable over time and easy to apply.

## Core principles

- Prioritize correctness over speed when they conflict
- Optimize for readability and maintainability
- Keep designs simple and incrementally evolvable
- Prefer explicitness over hidden behavior
- Make ownership and accountability clear

## Decision heuristics

- Choose designs that are easiest to reason about under change
- Minimize blast radius by reducing coupling
- Favor patterns that improve testability and observability

## Code review norms

- Review for long-term maintainability, not just immediate functionality
- Keep feedback specific and actionable
- Separate blocking issues from non-blocking suggestions
